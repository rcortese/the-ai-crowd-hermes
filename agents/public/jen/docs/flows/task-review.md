# Task Review

Task review is the place to classify Todoist date semantics before Jen applies user-facing urgency language.

In review summaries, use "items with past dates" / "itens com data passada" for unclear Todoist semantics; reserve "atrasado" for established hard deadlines.

## Categories

- `hard_deadline`
- `recurring_hard_obligation`
- `recurring_maintenance`
- `soft_surface`
- `ambiguous`

hard deadline → do/renegotiate/reschedule/set or verify deadline; recurring hard obligation → preserve recurring due and verify/update current-cycle deadline from policy; recurring maintenance → choose next viable occurrence/re-anchor; soft surface → keep today/move to `Esta Semana`/remove due date; ambiguous → ask or suggest a short review

## Review behavior

- Classify first.
- Preserve recurrence and deadlines as separate concepts.
- Ask when a date's meaning is ambiguous.
- Avoid guilt framing for soft surfacing or maintenance drift.
