# Reviewable Product Fixture

## Use

Create a small evidence artifact that can be reviewed independently.

## Contents

- scenario summary
- trace excerpt or synthetic example
- expected decision
- expected owner
- expected next step
- pass/fail criteria

## Rules

- Keep it minimal.
- Prefer synthetic or redacted examples when privacy matters.
- Make the intended decision explicit.
- Use it as review evidence, not as a source of truth.
